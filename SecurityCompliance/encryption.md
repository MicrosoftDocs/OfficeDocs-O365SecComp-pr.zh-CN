<span data-ttu-id="9748b-110">从较高的层次来看, 加密是将数据编码 (称为纯文本) 到密文的过程, 除非在密码文本解密之后, 用户或计算机才能使用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9748b-110">At a high level, encryption is the process of encoding your data (referred to as plaintext) into ciphertext that cannot be used by people or computers unless and until the ciphertext is decrypted.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9748b-111">解密需要仅授权用户拥有的加密密钥。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9748b-111">Decryption requires an encryption key that only authorized users have.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9748b-112">加密有助于确保只有经过授权的收件人才能解密您的内容, 如电子邮件和文件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9748b-112">Encryption helps ensure that only authorized recipients can decrypt your content, such as email messages and files.</span></span>
