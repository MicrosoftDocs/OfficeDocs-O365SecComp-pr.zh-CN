## <a name="inactive-mailboxes-and-ediscovery-case-holds"></a><span data-ttu-id="0c989-143">非活动邮箱和电子数据展示案例保留项</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c989-143">Inactive mailboxes and eDiscovery case holds</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0c989-p112">如果保留与安全中电子数据展示事例相关联的&amp;合规性中心置于邮箱和邮箱或删除用户的 Office 365 帐户，邮箱将变为非活动邮箱。但是，我们不建议使用电子数据展示事例包含进行非活动邮箱。这是因为电子数据展示事例适用于特定的时间限制的情况下相关法律问题。有时，法律案件可能将结束，将删除保留与案例相关联，并将关闭电子数据展示事例。在这一事实，如果放置在非活动邮箱的保留电子数据展示事例，与关联，然后释放保留项或电子数据展示案例是关闭 （或删除），将被永久删除非活动邮箱。此外，您无法创建基于时间的电子数据展示保留项。这就是的意味着不限次数或直到保留被删除，并删除非活动邮箱将保留在非活动邮箱中的内容。因此，建议为非活动邮箱使用诉讼保留或 Office 365 保留策略。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c989-p112">If a hold that's associated with an eDiscovery case in the Security &amp; Compliance Center is placed on a mailbox and then the mailbox or the user's Office 365 account is deleted, the mailbox will become an inactive mailbox. However, we don't recommend using eDiscovery case holds to make a mailbox inactive. That's because eDiscovery cases are intended for specific, time-bound cases related to a legal issue. At some point, a legal case will probably end and the holds associated with the case will be removed and the eDiscovery case will be closed. In fact, if a hold that's placed on an inactive mailbox is associated with an eDiscovery case, and then the hold is released or the eDiscovery case is closed (or deleted), the inactive mailbox will be permanently deleted. Additionally, you can't create a time-based eDiscovery hold. That's means content in an inactive mailbox will be retained forever or until the hold is removed and the inactive mailbox is deleted. Therefore, we recommend using a Litigation Hold or an Office 365 retention policy for inactive mailboxes.</span></span>
