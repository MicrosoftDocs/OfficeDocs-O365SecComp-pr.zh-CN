## <a name="what-eop-does-to-control-outbound-spam"></a><span data-ttu-id="27a99-119">控制出站垃圾邮件的 EOP</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-119">What EOP does to control outbound spam</span></span> 

1. <span data-ttu-id="27a99-120">将**出站流量分为不同 ip 池中的隔离流量**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-120">**Segregation of outbound traffic into separate pools of IPs**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-121">将扫描客户通过服务发送出站的每封邮件, 以查找垃圾邮件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-121">Every message that customers send outbound through the service is scanned for spam.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-122">如果邮件是垃圾邮件, 它将通过高风险传递池进行路由。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-122">If the message is spam, it is routed through the High Risk Delivery pool.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-123">此 IP 池包含未送达状态通知和垃圾邮件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-123">This IP pool contains non-deliverable status notifications and spam.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-124">不能保证传递给预期收件人, 因为由于它发出电子邮件的质量原因, 许多第三方不会接受电子邮件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-124">Delivery to the intended recipient is not guaranteed as many third parties will not accept email because the quality of email it emits.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="27a99-125">以这种方式拆分流量可确保较低质量的电子邮件 (垃圾邮件、退信 Ndr) 不会向下拖动常规出站电子邮件池的信誉。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-125">Splitting the traffic this way ensures that the lower quality email (spam, backscatter NDRs) does not drag down the reputation of the regular outbound email pools.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-126">在 Internet 周围的多个接收器上, 高风险池通常具有较低的声誉, 尽管这不是通用的。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-126">The high risk pool typically has low reputation at many receivers around the Internet, although this is not universal.</span></span> 

2. <span data-ttu-id="27a99-127">**监视 IP 信誉**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-127">**Monitoring of IP reputation**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-128">Office 365 查询各种第三方 IP blocklists, 如果其中列出了任何出站 IP, 将生成警报。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-128">Office 365 queries various 3rd party IP blocklists and generates alerts if any of our outbound IPs are listed on them.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-129">这使我们能够在垃圾邮件导致名誉下降时快速做出反应。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-129">This allows us to react quickly when spam has caused our reputation to degrade.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-130">生成警报时, 我们有内部文档外外部要采取什么步骤来获取 delisted。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-130">When an alert is generated, we have internal documentation outlying what steps to take to get delisted.</span></span> 

3. <span data-ttu-id="27a99-131">在**发送过多电子邮件标记为垃圾邮件时禁用违犯的帐户**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-131">**Disabling of offending accounts when they send too much email marked as spam**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-132">即使我们将垃圾邮件和非垃圾邮件隔离成两个单独的出站 IP 池, 电子邮件帐户也无法无限期发送垃圾邮件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-132">Even though we segregate our spam and non-spam into two separate outbound IP pools,  the email accounts cannot send spam indefinitely.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-133">我们会监视哪些帐户将发送垃圾邮件, 如果超过 undisclosed 限制, 将阻止该帐户发送垃圾邮件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-133">We monitor which accounts are sending spam and if it exceeds an undisclosed limit, the account is blocked from sending spam.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="27a99-134">标记为垃圾邮件的单个邮件可能是垃圾邮件引擎的 misclassification, 也称为误报。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-134">A single message marked as spam may be a misclassification by the spam engine and also known as a false positive.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-135">我们将通过高风险池发送它, 为其带来了机会。但是, 短时间内的大量邮件表示存在问题, 在发生此问题时, 将阻止该帐户发送更多的电子邮件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-135">We send it through the High Risk pool to give it a chance of going out; however, a large number of messages in a short time frame is indicative of a problem and when that occurs, we block the account from sending any more email.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-136">单个电子邮件帐户以及整个租户的聚合中存在不同的阈值。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-136">There are different thresholds that exist for individual email accounts as well as in aggregate for the entire tenant.</span></span>

4. <span data-ttu-id="27a99-137">在**过短时间内发送电子邮件过多的电子邮件时禁用违犯的帐户**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-137">**Disabling of offending accounts when they send too much email in too short a time frame**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-138">除了上述限制以查找标记为垃圾邮件的邮件的比例之外, 还会在达到总限制时阻止帐户, 而不管邮件是否被标记为垃圾邮件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-138">In addition to the limits above that look for a proportion of messages marked as spam, there are also limits that block accounts when they reach an overall limit regardless of whether or not the messages are marked as spam.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-139">此限制存在的原因是因为受到威胁的帐户可能会发送垃圾邮件筛选器错过的零天垃圾邮件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-139">The reason this limit exists is because a compromised account could send zero-day spam that is missed by the spam filter.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-140">由于很难 (如果不可能), 有时会告知合法大宗邮件市场活动与大量垃圾邮件市场活动之间的区别, 这些限制将激活, 以限制任何可能的损坏。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-140">Because it is difficult, if not impossible, to sometimes tell the difference between a legitimate mass mailing campaign and a massive spam campaign, these limits activate to limit any potential damage.</span></span>

> [!NOTE]
> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-141">对于 #3 和 #4, 我们不会公布确切的限制。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-141">For both #3 and #4, we do not advertise the exact limits.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="27a99-142">这是为了防止垃圾邮件制造者游戏系统, 并确保我们可以在需要时更改限制。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-142">This is to prevent spammers from gaming the system and to ensure that we can change the limits when we need to.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="27a99-143">这些限制值足够高, 因此一般的业务用户永远不会击中这些限制, 并且其不足以确保垃圾邮件制造者可以执行的大多数损坏。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="27a99-143">The limits are high enough such that an average business user will never hit them and low enough that it contains most of the damage a spammer can do.</span></span>
